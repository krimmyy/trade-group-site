Introduction

Edu is designed specifically for the higher education market as a tool to complement any course in international economics, or the economics of regional integration. It is useful for research students to provide contextual information and graphics for their research.

1. LOAD 2. ANALYSE 3. LEARN
Easily load your data on trade in goods, services or value-added trade, or data on tariffs into Edu. Quickly view and summarise your data in powerful pivot grids. Visualise your data with instant charts and tables. Analyse the data with a full set of built in indicator calculations. Create exercises for academic learning or use contextual information and graphics for your research and create empirical stories around key questions of trade.
