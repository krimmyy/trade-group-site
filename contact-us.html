Directions

Train

Falmer Railway Station is immediately opposite the University entrance. You may need to change at Brighton or Lewes main stations (about 8 minutes in either direction).

Bus

Bus number 25 runs between the centre of Brighton and the University – stopping at Churchill Square and Old Steine – bringing passengers directly onto the campus. In addition, bus services 28, 30, 129, 728 and 729 operate between Brighton (Old Steine) and destinations east of Falmer. They all stop right outside the University.

Car

The University is about 4 miles (6 km) outside Brighton or Lewes on the A27. If you are using Satillite Navigation, please enter our postcode BN1 9SB.
