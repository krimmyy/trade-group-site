Courses Introduction

Our trainers are experts in international trade and development economics who have significant experience in delivering tailored training courses in a wide range of contexts for both developed and developing countries.

Our courses range from 1 day up to 2 week courses, depending on what you require. We are also able to tailor training courses to explicitly suit you or your organisation’s needs.

At a glance, our training courses can incorporate the following:

  1. How to use the software.

  2. Trade in Value Added.

  3. Trade in Services.

  4. Partial Equilibrium Modelling.

  5. Gravity Modelling.

  6. Trade policy issues: regional trading agreements, export opportunity analysis, unilateral liberalization, preference erosion and deep integration.

  7. Understanding and using trade data, including explanations of data classification scheme and sources. Information on the downloading of data and use of your own national data.
